About US

The IskolarRealeño program is the Local Government Unit of Real, Quezon's commitment to supporting the educational aspirations of our community's growing student population. Recognizing the challenges associated with manual application and tracking processes, we developed this Scholarship Management Web System.

Our mission is to streamline the entire scholarship journey from application to monitoring ensuring accuracy, transparency, and operational efficiency so that educational assistance reaches those who need it most, faster and more effectively.
